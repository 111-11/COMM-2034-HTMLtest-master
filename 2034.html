Course Description:

Visual Media focuses on many different aspects of media incuding creating and editing videos with Preimere Pro, capturing photos and editing them with Photoshop, bulding different projects with Indesign and desinging your own website using Github. There aren't any exams or quizzes in this course just creative projects. You will advance your skills with technoolgy in many different areas. The course is weighted as Layout module 20%, Photo module 25%, Video module 20%, Web module 25% and Assignments 10%. The grading is A-F normal scale. The required textbook is White Space is Not You Enemy, by Golombisky and Hagen (2016). Focal Press.
