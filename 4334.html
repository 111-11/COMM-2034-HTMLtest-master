Course Description:

This course looks very in-depth at ethics in communication and particularly in the field of journalism. This course shows you many different examples of unethical issues and shines a light on what is ehtical. This course looks through a wide variety of philosphers who first introduced ehtics. In this course you give a group debate over an ethical issue and you write several papers on ethics. The course is weighted as participation: 30 percent, exams: 40 percent and team debate: 30 percent. In-class particpation is huge and taken in class almost everyday with different assignments that need to be turned in. The required texbook is Rosenstand, Nina (2017).The Moral of the Story: An introduction to ethics (Eighth edition). The grading is A-F normal scale. Overall you learn how to act ethically in a professional work enironment.
